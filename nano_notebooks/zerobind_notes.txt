zerobind: protein-specific zero-shot predictor

zero-shot = model preidcts properties on a protein it has never seen during training
few-shot = handful of examples (5-50) for a new protein before predicting

most proteins in ChEMBL have few known ligands so the model will constantly face proteins it barely knows
zero-shot performance will tell us whether the model learned chemistry or just memorized the well-studied targets

databases: BindingDB, CHEMBL, PDBbind, DrugBank
output = -log of kinetics (K_i, K_d, IC_50, EC_50)
