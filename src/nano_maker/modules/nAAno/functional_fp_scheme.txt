This is a handmade functional grouping scheme
For each amino acid where applicable, I've categorized them biochemically / organic chemistry style
I've then one-hot encoded these properties in nAAno_library.py
AAs that didn't have anything special going on (nonpolar) were all 0s, (non-)polarity is already tracked

1  Acidic R groups
    - D and E
2  Basic R groups
    - K, R, H
3  Guanidium
    - R
4  Imidazole (it's a different 5-membered ring so I didn't put this with 13)
    - H
5  Amide
    - N and Q
6  2S Bridge
    - C
7  Sulfur
    - M and C
8  Thiol
    - M
9  Aromatic
    - F, Y, W
10 Indole
    - W
11 Branched HC
    - L, I, V
12 Hydroxyl
    - Y, S, T
13 5-membered ring, 
    - P
